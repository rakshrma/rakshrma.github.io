I am a biomedical engineer who has been involved in multidisciplinary projects. During undergraduate degree, I fabricated a pressure sensor embedded contact lens. I collaborated with different research groups to synthesize osteoconductive bone cement for faster bone healing during my master's dissertation. I started my industrial experience by developing android application for disease diagnosis and then, worked with a start-up to optimize their microfluidics-based immunoassay platforms for hormone detection from blood. I also co-founded a surgical robotics startup to assist sugeons perform accurate intial puncture for laproscopy. In my current role as a data scientist at Eli Lilly, I am involved in multiple projects in collaboration with Purdue University. I have always been part of innovation and research. To learn from the pioneers in the field, and to witness most vibrat research ecosystem first-hand, I plan to pursue my doctorate.
